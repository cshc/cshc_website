Mens Teams

The club will make a contribution towards travel costs to men's away matches for players who drive and give a lift to and from the match venue to at least one of their team mates. A maximum of four cars should be used to give lifts. Payment will be made by team captains.

Ladies' Teams

Due to the shorter distances travelled by the ladies' teams, travel costs are recompensed through reductions in match fees for drivers.

Rates

The contribution is calculated at a rate of 15p a mile based on the round trip distance from Cambridge, rounded to the nearest 5 miles and 50p.
