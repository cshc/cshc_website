Juniors

Hockey training for boys and girls aged 6 - 12
on Saturday mornings

The youth section of Cambridge South Hockey Club train weekly on Saturday mornings during the hockey season. The sessions are for boys and girls aged 6 to 12. Sessions run from 9am to 10am and are led by professional coaches, with assistance from club members.

Training takes place at Long Road Sixth Form College.
