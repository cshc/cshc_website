CSHC Committee and Constitution

CSHC is a not-for-profit organisation run by its own members in accordance with the club Constitution. Prior to the 2009/10 season the Men's and Ladies' clubs were seperate entities but since then the two have merged and a single Committee now administers the club, with sub-committees for men's and ladies' playing matters. The Committee is appointed at the Annual General Meeting and appointments run for a single season.

You can see the Club Constitution here.
