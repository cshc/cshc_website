Ordering Kit

In addition to the playing shirts, the club also offers a variety of official playing and training kit available to order on our online club shop.

The playing kit items are all constructed out of high performance fabrics and are selected to complement your club shirt. The training wear is smart and practical and incorporates the club crest and personalised embroidery and printing options.
