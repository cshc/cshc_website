Playing shirts are custom manufactured for the Club by special order. New members are required to purchase a home shirt. These are provided at a subsidised price of £15, discounted from the full cost of £26. Away shirts are provided by the Club.

Ordering Kit

In addition to the playing shirts, the Club also offers a variety of official playing and training kit availble to order at  http://groupspaces.com/cambridgesouthhockeyclub/public/pay/shop/.

The playing kit items are all constructed out of high performance fabrics and are selected to complement your Club shirt. The training wear is smart and practical and again incorporates the Club crest and personalised embroidery and printing options.
