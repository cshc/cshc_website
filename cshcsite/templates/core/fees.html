New Players

If you are new to the club this season (i.e. did not pay an annual subscription last year), you can play a number of games before deciding whether to pay your annual subscription and join the club. Men who have played three games or ladies who have played two games will not subsequently be able to be selected until they have joined the club.

For players who join part way through the season, if you pay your annual subscription before the New Year then the normal annual subscription is due. If you pay after the New Year then you will pay 50% of the normal subscription plus 10% for each game played (i.e. up to 80%) before the New Year.

Of course, we're sure you'll be champing at the bit to join us and hand over your subscription after your first game!

Travel Costs

The Men's club will make a contribution towards travel costs to away matches for players who drive and give a lift to and from the match venue to at least one of their team mates. A maximum of four cars should be used to give lifts. Payment will be made by team captains.

Due to the shorter distances travelled by the Ladies' teams, travel costs are recompensed through reductions in match fees for drivers.

The contribution is calculated at a rate of 15p a mile based on the round trip distance from Cambridge, rounded to the nearest 5 miles and 50p. The table below shows the contribution which will be paid for trips to each pitch in our region.
