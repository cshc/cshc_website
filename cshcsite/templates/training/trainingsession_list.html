Training

Squad Training

Cambridge South runs professionally coached training sessions for men and ladies of all standards, every Monday evening during the season from September to March at The Leys School.

  • Mondays 7-9pm
  • The Leys School

Pay & Play

Cambridge South also offers informal weekly hockey on Tuesday evenings from 7:30pm to 8:30pm at Coldham's Common during the season.

A typical session consists of a 5 to 7 a-side game on a half pitch. The sessions are open to everyone, you don't have to be a club member to come along and new players are very welcome.

If you build up a thirst then there is usually a group of us going on for a drink or two afterwards at a nearby pub - a great way to get to know people in the club!

If you'd like to play, either contact {% clubinfo_email 'SecretaryEmail' sec_name %} any time during the week prior to the session you wish to attend or else just turn up at the pitch and introduce yourself. The cost is £4 or £2 for students and the unwaged. If you're new to the club, your first session is free!

  • Tuesdays 7:30-8:30pm
  • Coldham's Common
    (aka The Abbey)
