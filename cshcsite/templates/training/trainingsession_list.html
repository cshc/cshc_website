Training

Squad Training

Cambridge South runs professionally coached training sessions for men and ladies of all standards, every Monday evening during the season from September to March at The Leys School. Training for the 2013/14 season will start on 9th September 2013 - keep an eye on your emails for more updates.

  • Mondays 7-9pm
  • The Leys School

Summer Hockey

Cambridge South also offers informal weekly hockey on Thursday evenings from 8pm to 9pm at Wilberforce Road between April and August.

A typical session consists of a couple of 5 to 7 a-side games on half pitches, usually one mixed game and one men's game. The sessions are open to everyone, you don't have to be a club member to come along and new players are very welcome.

If you build up a thirst then there is usually a group of us going on for a drink or two afterwards at a nearby pub - a great way to get to know people in the club!

If you'd like to play, either contact {% clubinfo_email 'SecretaryEmail' sec_name %} any time during the week prior to the session you wish to attend or else just turn up at the pitch and introduce yourself. The cost is £4 or £2 for students and the unwaged. If you're new to the club, your first session is free!

  • Thursdays 8-9pm
  • Wilberforce Road
