Join Us

We are always looking for new members to join the club, whether experienced players or new to the sport!

If you're interested in joining or would just like more information about the club, simply fill in this form. Joining the club mailing lists means you will receive email alerts about games, training, socials etc.

Alternatively, come along to a training session - just turn up at Wednesday night pay-and-play (your first session is free) or Monday night training (always free!) and introduce yourself.

Fees

Details of club annual subscription fees and match fees can be found here. New players can play up to three matches for the men's teams or two matches for the ladies' teams before deciding whether to join.
