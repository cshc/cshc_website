Welcome to Cambridge South Hockey Club

We are a friendly, lively, Cambridge-based club who run four Men's and two Ladies' teams as well as Mixed and Indoor sides.

We offer something for everyone; whether you are experienced or new to the sport you will be very welcome at the club.

Join Cambridge South

We are always looking for new players to join us. Feel free to look around this site to find out more about each of the teams. Better still, why not attend the training on Monday evenings during the season at The Leys School, or our Pay and Play sessions at 8pm on Wednesday evenings at the Coldham's Common pitch or simply come along to watch a match live?
